About Us

Kuih Kekasih specialises in various traditional Malay Kuih. Made using authentic recipes passed down through many generations, you tastebuds will definitely not be disappointed. Using high quality ingredients, with minimal preservatives and prepared on a made-to-order basis, you can expect only the best and freshest quality bakes. Highly raved by various customers, with many loyal and returning customers on board. To date, we have served over 10,000 customers and have been featured on many news sites like Berita Harian and Resipi Nona. We take in orders of various sizes, be it intimate groups or for event catering.

Vision

We aim to withhold the tradition of Malay culture & delicacy of Kuih to Singapore and beyond. We want Kuih Kekasih to be the first thing people think of when they think of Malay Kuih.
